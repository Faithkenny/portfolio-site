Product design challenge - in the age of AI

I analyzed the brief using the 5W1H Product thinking framework. This means defining the knowns, and uncovering the unknowns. Some desktop research informed the hypotheses I'd make for the unknowns. I then charted out the userflow in Figjam. Finally, here's my process for using Figma Make, a GenAI visual design tool for building prototypes:

  • Prompt my mapped userflow,
  • Keep refining the prompt,
  • Select one outcome, & improve the visual polish in Figma
