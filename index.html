OKFN - Rebuilding an open source, non-linear workflow app

OKFN.org is a EU-based, Gov-tech nonprofit that advocates for Open knowledge through Data, community, & software. I was hired as the UX/UI Consultant to lead design while working alongside a cross-functional team of PMs and Engineers to solve the low adoption-rate problem, and retarget the app at a new usergroup: non-technical data professionals.

  • I delivered new dashboards, optimized workflows & a refreshed design system
  • One impact is this leading to a new free course on the ODE
  • Another is increased advocacy efforts through new training camps for Journalists

Designing for EU GDPR laws

Transcend.io is an Enterprise Data Governance platform based in SF, USA. Our customers have always had to deal with the laborious process of managing their ROPAs through spreadsheets. The Product goal is to build a new solution which significantly improves their experience & becomes another Company source of revenue.

  • Having a library of mental ux references was key here. I surely at the time didn’t know what Jakob’s Law said, however I had good pattern recognition abilities, and could link a defined problem with an unconventional example elsewhere.
  • This was an impactful project for the organization,as well as the design team. It became a key template file, as many custom components I design were pushed to the global design system.
